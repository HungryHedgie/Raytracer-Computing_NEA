FOREACH pixel IN screen
	Create several new ray objects of slightly different directions
	FOREACH ray IN currentPixelRays
		WHILE ray has not hit a light source
			FindIntersection
			Save info from Hit
			Find new direction
		END WHILE
		FOREACH hitInfo IN allIntersections
			calculate effect on final colour
		END FOREACH
		Combine all effects on final colour
	END FOREACH
	Take the average of each colour every ray returned
	Display that colour to the screen at that pixel coordinate
END FOREACH
